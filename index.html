Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get paid?
Payouts are processed every Friday. Once your clips are approved, earnings move to your available balance. Hit "Withdraw" on the Wallet page to send funds to your PayPal. Minimum withdrawal is $10.
How are earnings calculated?
Each campaign has a CPM rate (cost per 1,000 views). Your earnings = (views / 1,000) x CPM rate. Some campaigns also offer flat bonuses for viral clips. A 7% creator fee is deducted from your earnings.
How do I submit a clip?
Go to the Clips tab and tap "Submit Clip". Select the campaign, paste your video URL (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, or X), and submit. Our team reviews clips within 24-48 hours. You will get a notification when it is approved.
What is Trust Score?
Your Trust Score (0-100) reflects your reliability as a creator. It goes up when clips are approved and you follow campaign guidelines. Higher scores unlock priority access to new campaigns and faster payouts.
How do I join a campaign?
Browse available campaigns on the Discover page (accessible from Help > Discover). Tap "Join Campaign" and you will be accepted based on your Trust Score and creator profile. Most campaigns accept creators instantly.
Why was my clip rejected?
Common reasons: the video did not include the required brand mention or hashtag, it was shorter than the minimum length, or it violated campaign guidelines. Check the campaign requirements and resubmit a corrected version.
Can I submit to multiple campaigns?
Yes! You can join and submit clips to as many campaigns as you want. Each clip can only be submitted to one campaign, but you can create different clips for different campaigns using the same content style.
What platforms are supported?
We support TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and X (Twitter). Submit any public video URL from these platforms. Views are tracked automatically once your clip is approved.
